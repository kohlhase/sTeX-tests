A module that contributes a symvariant 
\begin{module}[id=BAR]
  \symdef{BAR}{BAR}
  \symdef[name=barbar]{barbarOp}{\psi}
  \symdef{barbar}[1]{\barbarOp(#1)}
  \symvariant{barbarOp}{cap}{\Psi}
  \symvariant{barbar}[1]{cap}{\barbarOp[cap](#1)}

  And it talks about these things a $\BAR$ and a $\barbar$ and its variant $\barbar[cap]$.
\end{module}
